Publications
 
Lan, Xiang, and Sanfeng Zhang. "MT-NCOR: A practical optimization method for network coded opportunistic routing in WMN." Computer Communication and Networks (ICCCN), 2014 23rd International Conference on. IEEE, 2014.
 
•
 
This paper thoroughly considers the channel capacity and interference between nodes in the deployment of NCOR based on 802.11 protocols, and proposes an NCOR protocol — MT-NCOR. MT-NCOR exploits the broadcast nature of 802.11 MAC and explores the multi-path diversity. Based on the optimization algorithm, MT-NCOR can perform fairly accurate rate control. Extensive experiments show that MT-NCOR can achieve significant throughout improvement and lower the transmission cost dramatically. 
Lan, Xiang, et al. "Analytic throughput model for TCP-NC." Wireless Communications and Networking Conference (WCNC), 2014 IEEE. IEEE, 2014.
 
•
 
This paper analyzes the evolutionary process of TCP-NC against lossy links. An analytic model is established by applying a two-dimensional Markov chain. With maximum window size, end-to-end erasure rate and redundancy parameter as input parameters, the analytic model can reflect window evolution and calculate end-to-end throughput of TCP-NC precisely. Base on the model, this paper also introduces a mechanism which improves the friendliness between coded streams and non-coded streams.
 
Lan, Xiang, Shuang Li, and Sanfeng Zhang. "Network Coded TCP throughput Analysis in Wireless Mesh Networks." Mobile Ad-hoc and Sensor Networks (MSN), 2013 IEEE Ninth International Conference on. IEEE, 2013.APA
 
•
 
This paper proposes a numerical analytic model base on three-dimensional Markov chain, which calculates TCP-NC throughput given end-to-end erasure rate and maximum window. This model can precisely predict the average maximum window and average throughput under a wide range of erasure rate, maximum window size and redundancy parameter.
